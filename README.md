# JanSeva AI

AI-Powered Smart Grievance Redressal System for Citizens and Government Departments

---

## Overview

**JanSeva AI** is an intelligent grievance management platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and government authorities.

The platform allows users to submit complaints regarding public issues such as:

* Road damage
* Water supply problems
* Electricity issues
* Garbage management
* Public safety concerns
* Infrastructure maintenance
* Civic administration complaints

Using Artificial Intelligence, JanSeva AI automatically:

* Analyzes the complaint
* Detects the issue category
* Assigns priority based on urgency
* Routes the complaint to the responsible government department
* Helps authorities focus on the most critical issues first

The goal of JanSeva AI is to make public grievance reporting faster, smarter, and more transparent.

# Key Features

## Powered by Gemini AI

JanSeva AI uses Google's Gemini AI model to intelligently process and understand citizen grievances.

The AI system helps in:

* Understanding natural language complaints
* Detecting urgency and severity
* Categorizing civic issues
* Mapping complaints to the correct departments
* Improving response efficiency

The system intelligently understands citizen complaints using Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques.

### Capabilities:

* Complaint classification
* Smart issue detection
* Department mapping
* Severity analysis
* Priority scoring

---

## Automatic Department Routing

JanSeva AI automatically forwards complaints to the correct authority.

Examples:

| Complaint Type       | Department                  |
| -------------------- | --------------------------- |
| Potholes / Roads     | Public Works Department     |
| Water Leakage        | Water Supply Department     |
| Street Light Failure | Electricity Department      |
| Garbage Issues       | Municipal Waste Management  |
| Public Safety        | Police / Emergency Services |

---

## Intelligent Priority System

Complaints are ranked based on urgency and public impact.

### Priority Levels:

* 🔴 Critical — Requires immediate attention
* 🟠 High — Important issue affecting many citizens
* 🟡 Medium — Standard grievance
* 🟢 Low — Minor issue

This helps government organizations focus on the most impactful problems first.

JanSeva AI introduces an automated AI workflow that:

1. Receives citizen complaints

3. Detects issue type and urgency

5. Routes the complaint to the correct government authority
